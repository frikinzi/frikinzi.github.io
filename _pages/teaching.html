My experiences as a woman of color in STEM motivated me to take on teaching roles throughout my academic career. I am thankful to all of my mentors that I've had who have inspired me to pursue graduate school.

I have taught in tutoring, research mentoring, outreach, and classroom settings since my time at Smith College. I ensure that my mentees are involved in my research, and they have coauthored papers with me.
